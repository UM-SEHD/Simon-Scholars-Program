Competition Overview

Strategy. Negotiation.
Innovation.

Participants pitch to a panel of industry judges across three challenge types designed to develop real-world skills in sport business. Instructors may assign specifics or allow students to choose their scenarios.

Judges evaluate each presentation holistically — weighing not just what you say, but how you say it, how you respond under pressure, and how creatively you solve the problem in front of you.

Visuals such as slides and charts are encouraged. All claims must be cited. Teamwork is evaluated where applicable.

Judging Criteria

  • 01 Content Relevance & Depth of Research
  • 02 Creativity & Original Thinking
  • 03 Feasibility & Realistic Projections
  • 04 Presentation Skills & Polish
  • 05 Teamwork & Collaboration

The Challenge

Corporate Sponsorship
Package Pitch

Women's sports are experiencing unprecedented growth in viewership, valuation, and sponsorship interest. You are the sales team. Your job is to walk into a boardroom, own the room, and close the deal.

Represent a professional women's sports property — a league, club, or individual team — in women's basketball or soccer. Identify one or more real target companies and build a pitch that earns their investment.

⚡ Key Constraint

The WNBA and NWSL are off-limits. Focus on emerging or smaller properties — Unrivaled, World Sevens, or similar — where data is sparse and creative strategy is essential. This is where real sales skill is built.

What Judges Are Looking For

The Six Dimensions
of a Winning Pitch

01

Persuasive Selling

Command attention, build belief, and guide the panel toward a yes. Clarity, confidence, and conviction are essential.

02

Objection Handling

Stay composed when challenged. Anticipate pushback and respond with logic, empathy, and data — not defensiveness.

03

Relationship Building

Sponsorship is a partnership. Demonstrate that you understand the sponsor's goals and can be a long-term ally.

04

Creative Activations

Include at least one innovative activation tailored to the sponsor's brand and the growing female fan base.

05

Financial Projections

Project realistic ROI using brand exposure metrics, lead generation estimates, and sales uplift modeling.

06

Presentation Polish

Every detail matters — visuals, pacing, language, and professionalism. Revenue maximization is the ultimate goal.

⭐ Bonus

Additional consideration will be given to teams targeting sponsors with prior investment in women's sports or publicly stated diversity and empowerment goals. Research deeply — this is where the extra point can make the difference.
